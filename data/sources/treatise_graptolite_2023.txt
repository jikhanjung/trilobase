reference: Maletz, J. (Ed.), 2023. Hemichordata. In: Selden, P.A. (Ed.), Treatise on Invertebrate Paleontology, Part V (Revised), Second Edition. Geological Society of America and University of Kansas Press
scope:
  - taxon: Graptolithina
    coverage: comprehensive
notes: |
  Major revision of Graptolithina classification. Graptolithina now treated as Subclass
  under Class Pterobranchia (not a separate class). Orders Tuboidea, Camaroidea,
  Stolonoidea of Bulman (1955, 1970) dissolved; genera redistributed.
  Graptoloidea dramatically reorganized with suborders Graptodendroidina,
  Sinograptina, Dichograptina, Glossograptina, Axonophora.
  Multiple authors per section: Maletz (most sections), Mitchell & Melchin
  (Normalograptidae), Lenz & others (Retiolitidae), Maletz & Loydell (Monograptidae).
  Uncertain genera (nomina dubia, non-graptolite taxa) listed but not included in
  the main classification.
